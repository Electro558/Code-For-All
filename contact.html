Frequently Asked Questions

How much time commitment is required?

We have weekly meetings that last about an hour. Additional time commitments vary based on your role and the events we're planning. General members typically commit 2-3 hours per week, while leadership positions may require 4-5 hours weekly. We understand that academics come first and work with members to balance their commitments.

Do I need previous volunteer experience?

No previous experience is required! We welcome members of all experience levels. Our club is a great place to learn about philanthropy and community service. We provide training and mentorship for all new members.

Can I join mid-semester?

Yes! While we have our main recruitment at the beginning of each semester, we accept new members year-round. Simply fill out the application form on this page, and we'll get back to you with information about our next meeting.

How are the causes we support selected?

We select causes through a democratic process. Members can propose causes they're passionate about, and we vote as a club on which initiatives to pursue each semester. We typically focus on 2-3 main causes per semester to maximize our impact.

Do you provide community service hours?

Yes! We track all volunteer hours for our members. These hours can be used for graduation requirements, honor society applications, college applications, and more. We provide official documentation of your service hours at the end of each semester.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join our club today and be part of something meaningful. Together, we can create positive change in our community!
