Our Mission

Code For All is dedicated to fostering a spirit of coding, technology education, and digital literacy among students. We believe that young people have the power to create innovative solutions to real-world problems through programming and technology.

Through coding workshops, hackathons, mentorship programs, and community tech projects, we aim to make programming accessible to everyone and build a more inclusive tech community.
